### Coracobrachialis

The corocobrachialis originates from the corocoid process of the [[Jethro’s Working Notes/Scapulae\|Scapulae]] and inserts at the medial side of the midshaft of the [[Jethro’s Working Notes/Humerus\|Humerus]].
