### Lesser omentum

The lesser omentum is a peritoneal fold that extends from the lesser curvature of the stomach and the first part of the duodenum to the inferior part of the liver. The lesser omentum is anterior of the lesser sac of the [[Jethroâ€™s Working Notes/Peritoneal cavity\|Peritoneal cavity]].

- **The lesser omentum is separated into 3 parts:**
    1. Hepatoduodenal ligament - it conveys the hepatic triad
    2. Hepatogastric ligament
    3. Hepatoesophageal ligament
