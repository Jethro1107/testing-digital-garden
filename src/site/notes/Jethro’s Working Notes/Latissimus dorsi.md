### Latissimus dorsi

Latissimus dorsi originates from the spinous process of T7 to T12 and the thoracolumbar fascia. It courses superiorly and anteriorly, where it twists and inserts into the floor of the intertubercular groove: [[Jethroâ€™s Working Notes/Lady M lies between two majors\|Lady M lies between two majors]].

- ****************************************************************Movement of the latissimus dorsi****************************************************************
    
    It enables adduction of the shoulder joint.
    
    It enables extension of the shoulder joint.
    
    It enables medial rotation of the shoulder joint.
